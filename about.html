Who We Are

More Than a
Dealership

Bro Cars was founded in 2012 by Arjun Perera, a lifelong car enthusiast with a bold vision: make buying a car an experience people celebrate rather than endure. Starting with six vehicles in a modest Colombo showroom, Arjun built the business on three non-negotiable principles — honesty, quality, and genuine care.

Over twelve years, we've grown into Sri Lanka's most recognized automotive brand — 8,000 sq ft showroom, a 25-strong team, and over 500 vehicles sold annually. Yet despite our scale, every customer still feels like family. Because that's the Bro Cars way.

We specialize in premium European and Japanese vehicles sourced from verified importers and private sellers. Every car is photographed, inspected, and priced transparently. You deserve the full picture before making a major decision — and we make sure you have it.
